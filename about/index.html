Most published “PPC tools” lists are 10 tools deep, organized by ranking. The reality is the modern PPC stack runs across 8+ functional categories, with 60+ tools that earn consideration depending on your specific stack architecture. The directory here is built for advertisers actually building stacks, not browsing rankings.

Why an encyclopedia, not a list

An encyclopedia organizes by job-to-be-done. You come looking for a tool that does X; the directory shows you the 4–6 tools that do X well, with annotations on which one fits which spend tier or buyer persona. That’s the discovery mode that matches how stacks actually get built.

The stack templates

Five prebuilt stack templates live on the methodology page — solo agency, in-house at $100K MRR, ecom Shopping-heavy, B2B lead gen, enterprise. Each is a curated 5–8 tool stack with reasoning. They’re the equivalent of “starter packs” for common situations.

What I won’t do

Sponsored placements, affiliate-for-ranking arrangements — declined.
